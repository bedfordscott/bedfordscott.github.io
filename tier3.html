Tier 3 - Increased Anonymity

Enhance your online privacy and anonymity with these additional tools and practices.

Virtual Private Networks (VPNs)

A VPN encrypts your internet connection and masks your IP address, making it harder for websites and third parties to track your online activity.

  • ExpressVPN - A reliable and user-friendly VPN service with a strong focus on privacy and security.
  • Private Internet Access - An affordable VPN service with a large server network and robust privacy features.
  • ProtonVPN - A secure VPN service provided by the team behind ProtonMail, offering a free tier and strong encryption.

When choosing a VPN, consider factors such as privacy policy, server locations, speed, and ease of use. Always enable your VPN when browsing the web to protect your privacy.

Privacy-focused Search Engines

These search engines prioritize user privacy by not tracking or profiling users, and they do not store search history or personal information.

  • DuckDuckGo - A privacy-focused search engine that does not track users or personalize search results.
  • Startpage - A search engine that delivers Google search results without tracking or logging user data.

By using these privacy-focused search engines instead of Google or Bing, you can reduce your digital footprint and protect your search history from being collected and analyzed.

Anonymous Email Addresses

Creating anonymous or disposable email addresses for less important accounts can help protect your primary email address from spam, phishing attempts, and data breaches.

  • ProtonMail - An encrypted email service that offers anonymous accounts and focuses on privacy and security.
  • GuerrillaMail - A disposable email service that provides temporary email addresses for one-time use.

Use anonymous or disposable email addresses when signing up for services or accounts that do not require your real identity, and reserve your primary email for important communications.

Encrypted Cloud Storage

Encrypted cloud storage services protect your data by encrypting it before it leaves your device, ensuring that only you can access your files.

  • Tresorit - A secure cloud storage service with end-to-end encryption and a user-friendly interface.
  • Sync.com - Another encrypted cloud storage provider that offers secure file sharing and collaboration features.

When storing sensitive data in the cloud, choose a provider that offers strong encryption and a clear privacy policy to ensure your data remains confidential.
