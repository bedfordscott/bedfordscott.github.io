Tier 1 - Basic Privacy Protection

Start your online privacy journey with these simple steps that anyone can take.

Privacy-focused Web Browsers

These browsers prioritize user privacy by blocking trackers, not saving browsing history, and minimizing data collection.

  • Firefox - An open-source browser with strong privacy features and customizable settings.
  • Brave - A Chromium-based browser that blocks ads and trackers by default, and rewards users with BAT cryptocurrency.
  • DuckDuckGo - A privacy-focused browser and search engine that doesn't track user activity or collect personal information.

Social Media Privacy Settings

Adjust your social media account settings to control who can see your posts, profile information, and activity.

  • Facebook Privacy Settings - Guide to manage your Facebook privacy settings and control your visibility on the platform.
  • Twitter Privacy Settings - Instructions to make your Twitter account private, limiting who can see your tweets and interact with you.
  • LinkedIn Privacy Settings - Overview of LinkedIn privacy settings to control your profile visibility and data sharing.

Encrypted Messaging Apps

These apps use end-to-end encryption to secure your conversations and protect your messages from being intercepted by third parties.

  • Signal - A free, open-source messaging app with strong encryption and privacy features, recommended by security experts.
  • WhatsApp - A widely-used messaging app owned by Facebook that offers end-to-end encryption for text messages, voice calls, and video chats.

End-to-end encryption ensures that only the intended recipients can read your messages, preventing access by the app provider, government agencies, or hackers.

Be Cautious About Sharing Personal Information Online

Limit the amount of personal data you share on public websites and social media. Avoid posting sensitive information like your full address, phone number, or financial details.

Remember, once something is posted online, it can be difficult or impossible to remove completely. Think carefully before sharing any personal information that could be used to identify or target you.
